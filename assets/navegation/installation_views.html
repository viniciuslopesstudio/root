Vinícius Lopes: No outro dia, Mármol Project, 23 November - 14 December 2023

No outro dia is like a diffuse but present memory, it is the response of a tired mind that does not forget. It may be the story of our origin and uprooting. It will haunt us in our jobs, in our habits, in our languages and in our ways of relating to each other. It will remain present in our memory, the memory that changed and changes us. No outro dia could confirm us that the idea of freedom will not replace the memory of all that we have lost.

Vinícius Lopes: Descançaço, OKNA Cultural Space, 15 June - 2 July 2023
