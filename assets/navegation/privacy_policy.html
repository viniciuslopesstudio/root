Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to collect information about how you use our website. This includes your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and the time and date of your visit.

Use of Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device to help us improve your experience on our website. We use the following types of cookies: Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of the website. Analytics Cookies: We use Google Analytics to track your usage of our website, which helps us understand how you interact with our site and improve it. Preloading Cookies: These cookies help us preload images to ensure faster loading times and a better user experience.
